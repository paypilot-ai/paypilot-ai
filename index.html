📖 How to Use PayPilot
Everything you need to get up and running — step by step, plain and simple.
✅ Quick Start — Do These First
1
Go to Settings
Enter your name, company name, and select your default tone. This personalizes every AI response.
2
Load your script
Go to Scripts, pick an opening line and rebuttals for common objections. The AI uses these on every call.
3
Make your first call
Use Live Call Assist for manual calls or AI Auto-Caller for fully automated outreach.
🏠
Dashboard
Your home base

See your call activity, recent wins, and a summary of your plan at a glance. This is the first screen you'll see when you log in. Nothing to set up here — it updates automatically.

🤖
Live Call Assist
AI coaches you in real time while you're on the call

You make the call. The AI listens and gives you the perfect thing to say — right when you need it.

How to use it:
1. Enter the customer's phone number and click Call Now.
2. Click the 🎤 mic button — PayPilot starts listening to the call.
3. When the customer raises an objection, the AI types a suggested response automatically.
4. Click 📞 Send to Call — the AI speaks your response to the customer.
5. If the call closes, click Send Agreement to email the contract instantly.
💡 Tip: If the customer speaks Spanish, Portuguese, or French — PayPilot auto-detects it, translates what they say to English so you can hear it, and replies to them in their language automatically.
📞
AI Auto-Caller
The AI makes the call — no rep needed

The AI dials the number, introduces itself, listens to the prospect, handles objections, and closes — completely on its own.

How to use it:
1. Fill in Your Company Name, Customer Name, and Phone Number.
2. In Reason for Call, write a short note — e.g. "Follow up on insurance quote" or "Introduce B2B payment program."
3. Optionally attach an agreement to send if the call closes.
4. Click 🤖 Launch AI Call — the AI takes it from there.
💡 Tip: The more specific your "Reason for Call," the better the AI performs. Instead of "sales call," try "Enroll them in our ACH direct deposit program — they currently pay by check."
📝
Scripts
Pre-built responses for every situation

Scripts give the AI a foundation to work from. Choose an opening line, select rebuttals for common objections (fees, timing, security, etc.), and the AI weaves them into natural-sounding responses. You can customize or add your own lines at any time.

📊
Analytics
Track your performance over time

See how many calls you've made, what objections come up most, and where deals are won or lost. Use this to improve your pitch and know which customers to follow up with.

⚙️
Settings
Personalize your workspace

Set your name, company name, preferred AI tone (Confident, Warm, Direct, or Calm), and default call language. These settings shape how the AI sounds on every call — set them once and you're good to go.

🛡️
Compliance
Stay legal on every call

PayPilot automatically plays a recording disclosure at the start of every call so you're covered. The Compliance Center lets you review your settings, manage your do-not-call list, and confirm your call recording laws are set for your state.

❓ Common Questions
Do I need to be on the phone for the AI Auto-Caller? ▾

No. The AI handles the entire conversation without you. You just fill in the details, hit Launch, and check back later to see how it went.

What if the customer speaks Spanish? ▾

In Live Call Assist, PayPilot detects the language automatically. You'll hear the customer's words translated to English through your browser, and the AI will reply to the customer in Spanish. No setup needed.

How do I send a contract after a call? ▾

In the Live Call Assist panel, enter the customer's email and attach your agreement file (PDF, Word, or image). When the deal closes, click Send Agreement — it emails the document to the customer instantly.

Can I change how the AI sounds? ▾

Yes. Go to Settings and pick your tone: Confident (direct, assertive), Warm (friendly, empathetic), Direct (straight to the point), or Calm (patient, measured). You can also change the tone mid-call from the Call Assist panel.

Is the call being recorded? ▾

Yes. All calls are recorded for training and quality purposes. A disclosure is automatically played at the start of every call to notify the customer, keeping you compliant.
